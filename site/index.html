About QTB

A Family Business.
An Industrial Operation.

Quality Truck Bodies & Repair, Inc. has been manufacturing custom truck bodies in Elm City, North Carolina since 1970. What started as a commitment to building better truck bodies has grown into a full-service operation spanning custom fabrication, equipment distribution, and repair.

Our facility houses dedicated repair bays and a complete fabrication and assembly shop. As industries have grown and shifted over the decades, we've adapted — adding new capabilities, partnering with brands like Reading Truck, and expanding our service offerings to meet whatever our customers need.

We're still family-owned, still hands-on, and still building every body like our name is on it — because it is.
